Who is Cristiano Ronaldo?


Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro born February 5th 1985, is the greatest Soccer player of all time.

He hails from Funchal, Madeira Portugal. He currently plays the position of forward for his boyhood club of manchester United.

His career accolades and broken records all testify to the fact that without a doubt and by every measure, Cristiano Ronaldo is the greatest soccer player to ever touch the field.
